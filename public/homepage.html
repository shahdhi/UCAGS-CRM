What Our Platform Does

An all-in-one system built for our admissions, academic, and operations teams.

🎓

Lead & Admissions Management

Track prospective student enquiries, follow-ups, and admissions progress from initial contact to enrolment.

📋

Registrations & Enrolments

Manage student registrations, batch assignments, and course enrolment records in one place.

💳

Payment Tracking

Monitor instalment payments, generate receipts, and keep accurate financial records for all students.

📅

Attendance & Calendar

Record student attendance, manage leave requests, and schedule tasks and follow-ups on a shared calendar.

💬

WhatsApp Communications

Send and receive WhatsApp messages with students and leads directly within the platform.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Generate daily checklists, performance reports, and data exports for management decision-making.

🔒 How We Use Google Services

Our platform integrates with Google Workspace to improve productivity for authorised UCAGS staff members only. Specifically, we use:

  • Google Sheets — to sync lead and student data with shared internal spreadsheets used by our admissions officers.
  • Google Drive — to store and retrieve documents related to student records and batch management.
  • Google Calendar — to create and manage follow-up tasks and scheduling reminders for staff.
  • Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0) — to allow authorised staff to securely authenticate with their institutional Google accounts.

We only access the minimum data required to perform these functions. We do not share, sell, or distribute any Google user data to third parties. All access is limited to verified UCAGS staff accounts.

1 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Universal College of Applied & General Studies ("UCAGS", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the UCAGS Student Management System (the "Platform").

The Platform is an internal, staff-only system used exclusively by authorised UCAGS employees (admissions officers, academic coordinators, and administrators). It is not a public-facing consumer product. By accessing the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

4 Google User Data

The Platform integrates with Google Workspace APIs. When a staff member connects their Google account, we may access and use the following Google user data:

  • Google Profile (email, name) — used to authenticate the staff member and personalise their session. We do not store Google profile pictures beyond the active session.
  • Google Sheets — read and write access to specific internal spreadsheets used to manage lead and student records, and batch data shared with admissions officers.
  • Google Drive — create, read, and organise files in UCAGS-owned Drive folders for student documentation and batch management.
  • Google Calendar — create and manage calendar events for follow-up tasks and scheduling reminders for staff members.

We commit to the following regarding Google user data:

  • We access Google user data only for the purposes described above and for no other purpose.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share Google user data with any third party, advertiser, or data broker.
  • We do not use Google user data to serve advertisements.
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data except where (a) you have explicitly given permission, (b) it is necessary for security purposes, or (c) we are required to do so by law.
  • Google OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only to facilitate the specific Google Workspace integrations described in this Policy.
  • Our use of Google API services complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5 How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and authorise staff access to the Platform.
  • To manage student and prospective student records (leads, registrations, payments, attendance).
  • To sync data between the Platform and Google Sheets / Drive for collaborative staff workflows.
  • To schedule and manage follow-up tasks via Google Calendar.
  • To send and record WhatsApp communications with students and leads.
  • To generate reports, daily checklists, and analytics for management.
  • To ensure platform security, prevent fraud, and audit access logs.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

6 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal data to any third party. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within UCAGS: Data is accessible to authorised UCAGS staff members on a need-to-know basis.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database hosting) and Vercel (cloud hosting). These providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements and may not use the data for their own purposes.
  • Google Workspace: Data is shared with Google APIs as described in Section 4, solely to enable the integrations.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, data may be transferred to the successor entity.

7 Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy:

  • Staff account data: Retained for the duration of employment and up to 3 years after leaving, or as required by law.
  • Student & lead data: Retained for up to 7 years from the date of last interaction, in accordance with educational record-keeping requirements.
  • Google OAuth tokens: Retained while the staff member's Google integration is active. Tokens are revoked and deleted promptly when a staff member disconnects their Google account or leaves the organisation.
  • Logs: Server and access logs are retained for up to 12 months.

8 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Hashed password storage (plaintext passwords are never stored).
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorised staff only.
  • Secure storage of OAuth tokens with least-privilege scopes.
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring of access logs.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. If you suspect a security incident, please contact us immediately at info@ucags.lk.

9 Your Rights

Authorised users of the Platform have the following rights with respect to their personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Revoke Google Access: You may revoke the Platform's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account Permissions page. This will disable Google Workspace integrations for your account.
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@ucags.lk.

10 Cookies

The Platform uses the following cookies and local storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookies: Required for maintaining your authenticated session. These are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Supabase auth tokens: Stored in browser local storage to maintain your login state across sessions.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

11 Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for use by adult UCAGS staff members only. It is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child's data has been processed, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify authorised users via the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
